DECO1800 - Convict Lyfe

DECO1800 - Convict Lyfe

What was the project?

Using standard HTML5 and CSS3 as well as vanilla javascript and jQuery, my team and I were tasked with conceptualising, refining and implementing a web-based application from data collected by the State Library of Queensland.

We created an educational tool aimed at teaching young primary school students about the life and history of Australian convicts. We created a click adventure that allowed users to explore as different convicts and earn their freedom by answering quiz questions. We aimed to create an immersive personalisable educational app that encouraged student learning.
